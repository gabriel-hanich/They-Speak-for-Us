They Speak for Us

How does the press cover stories that matter to you?

The media speaks for the public. It should both tell us reliable information, free from systemic bias and corruption.They Speak for Us is using big data to understand long-term trends in current and historical coverage of all news stories

How does it work?

They Speak for Us allows you to see how media outlets from across the globe cover events, topics and stories. The articles are collected via each outlets' RSS feed, and can be viewed in our data explorer. Most of the selected outlets focus on politics, however current affairs and pop culture articles are also present

The database has close to 100% coverage of outlets for articles published between 01/03/2022 and now
